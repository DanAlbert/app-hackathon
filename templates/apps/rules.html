Rules:

1. Keep your app family friendly.

2. Cannot have any pornographic material.

3. Do not copy any copyrighted material.

4. Do not sabotage any other team's code.

5. The apps must be demonstrable (Make something that people can witness). Showing that you made a Turing complete circuit board is not demonstrable. Showing an app that shoots birds through the air is demonstrable.
